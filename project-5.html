01 THE CHALLENGE

Construction and repair field teams faced a critical sales conversion bottleneck: generating accurate quotes and contracts required manual back-office processing that took hours or days, causing potential clients to lose interest or choose competitors who could respond faster:

  • Slow Quote Turnaround: Field staff captured client requirements on-site but had to wait for office staff to calculate pricing and generate proposals
  • Lost "Hot" Clients: Critical decision window closed while waiting for quotes, with prospects choosing faster-responding competitors
  • Manual Estimation Errors: Human calculation mistakes led to underpricing (lost profit) or overpricing (lost deals)
  • Inconsistent Quoting: Different field staff and estimators produced varying quotes for similar work
  • Messy Input Data: PDF drawings, photos, and partial documentation arrived in inconsistent formats
  • Complex Back-and-Forth: Multiple rounds of clarification between field staff, clients, and office created delays and frustration

The company needed a field-ready AI system that could understand messy inputs (PDF drawings, photos, verbal descriptions), guide crews through structured data capture, calculate accurate quotes automatically, and produce professional contract documents‚Äîall while the client was still on-site and engaged.
