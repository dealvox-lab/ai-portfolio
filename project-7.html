01 THE CHALLENGE

A brokerage company's outbound sales team faced systematic obstacles reaching decision-makers: IVR menu systems, voicemail screening, and gatekeeper friction blocked most connection attempts, while manual calling workflows created inefficiency and inconsistent follow-up:

  • IVR Navigation Barriers: Complex phone menu systems blocked access to decision-makers, requiring human agents to manually navigate menus
  • Voicemail Detection Gaps: Inability to distinguish between voicemail systems and live answers led to wasted agent time and poor call classification
  • Gatekeeper Friction: Receptionists and assistants screening calls with questions like "what is this regarding?" created additional conversion barriers
  • Manual Call Classification: Sales reps manually logging call outcomes (no answer, voicemail, connected, qualified) consumed valuable selling time
  • CRM Sync Delays: Lag between call completion and CRM updates prevented real-time prioritization and follow-up routing
  • Scalability Limits: Human-dependent outreach couldn't scale to thousands of call attempts without proportional headcount increases

The company needed an AI voice agent that could autonomously navigate IVR systems, detect and classify call outcomes (voicemail, no answer, live connection), handle initial gatekeeper interactions, and automatically sync structured call intelligence into Dynamics 365‚Äîall while operating at scale without manual supervision.

02 SOLUTION ARCHITECTURE

Built a production AI calling system combining voice conversation engine, IVR navigation logic, voicemail detection, webhook-driven event processing, and Dynamics 365 synchronization to automate high-volume outbound calling with intelligent decision-making.
